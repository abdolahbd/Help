How to use it

Instant Preview on Hover

The panel lists every message in your conversation as sequential items (e.g., Q1, A1, Q2, A2…). Hovering your mouse over any list item instantly displays a preview of that message. This allows you to quickly recall the context without scrolling through a long chat.

One-Click Scroll Navigation

Need to jump to a particular question or answer? Simply click the corresponding item in the panel, and the extension automatically scrolls the main chat window to that exact spot. This helps you locate and revisit important parts of your conversation without any hassle.
